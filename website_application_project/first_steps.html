Thinking about getting a Dog?

So you've finally put your paws on the trigger... you want a fury friend for you or your family. Before the fun there are a few things we'll need to get ready first. First know your breed, if you're looking for a specific breed or not every dog is diffrent. While they all start out as cute puppies a lasa hapso will grow and behave diffrently than a lab. More factors include temperment, activity levels, sheading and grooming, and even alergies.

With a breed secured and reserched basic supplies will need to be bought this includes bowels (automatic or manuel) for food. A bed for napping and some toys for playing, although most dogs will probably be alittle shy on the first day. Identification tags and flea collars are a must as well as a inital vet appointment. Lastly understand that a dog is more than just a pet its a part of the family and conciderations for potential emergencies, insurence, and gromming if you dont do it yourself should be planned for.
