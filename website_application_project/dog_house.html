Sleeping habits

Your dog being hyperactive or extremely sluggish could be more than just iorregular sleeping patterns, here are some thing you might want to know. Dont be worried about long sleeping times, while humans usually get 8 hours a day dongs can sleep for a total 14 hours a day, over 18 for puppies. Like alot of things the size and breed of the dog affects its sleeping pattern, larger dogs sleep more while smaller dogs sleep less however working dogs that are more active sleep less regardless. Daily naps are natual wether due to bordom or low energy levels dont try and keep them awake daily rest is important to them. Consult a veterenarian if their established pattern changes suddenly or they being to show constant signs or lethargy.

Dog gates and off limit areas

Dogs are curios, natral exploreres, lets make sure they dont discover something dangerous. While cleaning up choking hazards and tripping areas are the main reasonns others also come when limiiting a dogs accsess to certin areas. To reduce begging and dog hair in food limiting acsess to the kitchen is a must and to stop the taring up of trash and or importand documents the bathroom and office areas should also be off limits. For dogs that are to yong for training physical barriers like gates and or certin sprays can be used to limit exploration, sprays can also be used to discuage the chewing of cabinates and furnature. When training comes in start small with "sit and stay training" with a reward for completion then walking up to the area and stopping before you enter, rewarding them if they stop to. Ensure your dog has a space for themselves, with sun toys and a bed traditional or cave like.

Dogs Otside vs Inside

Should your dog be outside or inside?
Dogs for the most part should stay inside when not using the bathroom or walking for play / exersise due to many reasons. One is untrained or young dogs may wander off if the area isnt secured or may chase another dog or animal causing them to get lost. Another factor against outdoor living is envormental factors like temprature may be too hot or cold for certin breeds, for dogs with thick fur and no anti flea protection may develop them and or parasites from outside. Lastly dogs are at their core socail creatures and requiured interaction through playing, grooming, or just being beside their owner. Without this they may start to exhibit behavior problems like aggrassion or barking. In the very rare case that a dog has to stay outside they'll require a new set activities tailored to their expirience pescribed by a licenced veterenarian and will require regular interaction.
