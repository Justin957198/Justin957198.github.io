Welcome and Overview

Welcome to Raising Canines a hobby / informative website on raising dogs, here we'll learn the basics of pet care, housing, training and more from a fellow pet owner. Whether your a first time pet owner of a season fur parent this site is guaranteed to add to your knowledge of dog care. For those wanting to adopt but are unsure of the process / preparations the First trot page is for you with all the basics in popular dog breeds and home prep. Then travel to The Dog House for home management and potty training which Segway's nicely into the Training station for information on dog training. Lastly this website comes with a Dog board, just use the sign up form upload a picture of your dog and some notes and you'll be part of this wonderful site.
